Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students:

DO's:
1) Maintain a professional profile.
2) Use strong and unique passwords.
3) Think before posting anything online.
4) Respect others on social media.
5) Keep personal information secure.

DON'Ts:
1) Do not share private or sensitive information.
2) Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
3) Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
4) Do not use weak passwords.
5) Avoid engaging in cyberbullying.
