Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

1) Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
2) Always verify website URLs before entering personal details.
3) Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
4) Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5) Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
6) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or payments.
7) Regularly update your apps and devices for security.
8) Be cautious while downloading files or apps from unknown sources.

UPI/Financial Safety:
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests

Reporting:
- Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 immediately in case of fraud.
